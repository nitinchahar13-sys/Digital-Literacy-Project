📄 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)

✅ Actionable Safety Tips
✔ Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts (mix of letters, numbers, symbols)
✔ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps
✔ Never share OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank
✔ Always verify UPI collect requests before approving—remember, receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN
✔ Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links sent via SMS, email, or messaging apps
✔ Download apps only from official app stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store
✔ Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into banking or sensitive accounts
✔ Regularly update your apps and device software to fix security vulnerabilities
✔ Adjust privacy settings on social media to control who can see your personal information
✔ Log out from accounts when using shared or public computers


💳 Special Focus: UPI / Financial Safety
✔ Double-check the receiver’s name and ID before making any payment
✔ Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept unexpected payment requests


🚨 Report Cybercrime in India

If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately:

🌐 National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
