Social Media Checklist (For College Students):

✅ Do’s (Responsible Practices)
✔ Think before you post; ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
✔ Maintain privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information
✔ Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
✔ Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
✔ Engage positively and respectfully with others online


❌ Don’ts (Things to Avoid)
✘ Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords)
✘ Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
✘ Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
✘ Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown sources
✘ Do not spend excessive time on social media that affects your studies
